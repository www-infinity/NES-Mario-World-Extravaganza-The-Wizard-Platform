NINTENDO HISTORY

🗓️ THE NES STORY (1983–1995)

Nintendo released the Famicom in Japan on July 15, 1983, and the Nintendo Entertainment System in North America on October 18, 1985. It saved the video game industry after the crash of 1983 and established Nintendo as the dominant force in gaming.

Key milestones: Donkey Kong (1981), Super Mario Bros. (1985), The Legend of Zelda (1986), Metroid (1986), Mega Man (1987), Contra (1987), Tetris (1984), and hundreds more.

🎵 KOJI KONDO — THE MAESTRO

Koji Kondo composed the iconic music for Super Mario Bros., The Legend of Zelda, and many other Nintendo classics using the NES's 5-channel audio chip (2A03).

His Super Mario Bros. theme is the most recognized piece of video game music in history, recognized by over 97% of Americans in surveys. The underwater theme, castle theme, and underground theme are equally legendary.

🌟 MARIO THROUGH THE AGES

From Jumpman in Donkey Kong (1981) to Super Mario Bros. (1985), Super Mario Bros. 3 (1988), and beyond — Mario has starred in over 200 games, sold over 800 million copies worldwide, and become the world's most recognizable video game character.

Miyamoto's masterpiece continues to evolve while honoring its 8-bit roots.

GAME INSIGHTS & PRO TIPS

🔴 SUPER MARIO BROS.

WARP ZONES: In World 1-2, run on top of the exit pipe area ceiling to reach the warp zone for Worlds 2, 3, or 4.

INFINITE LIVES: In 3-1, find the two Goombas near the staircase and bounce on them repeatedly with the correct timing for 1-up stacks.

MINUS WORLD: In 1-2, jump through the wall to the left of the warp zone pipe for World -1 (an infinite underwater level).

⭐ SUPER MARIO BROS. 3

WARP WHISTLES: World 1-3 (White block secret), World 1 Fortress (fly past the boss room), World 2 Toad House hint.

P-WING: Fly through the entire level without landing once.

TANUKI SUIT: Statue form grants invincibility for 5 seconds — great for boss rooms.

CARD GAME: Matching N-cards gives you 2, 3, or 5 extra lives depending on the match.

🗡️ THE LEGEND OF ZELDA

2ND QUEST: Enter the name "ZELDA" to start on the harder second quest immediately.

MONEY TRICK: Gamble your money, then hold reset when you lose to restore it.

LEVEL 9 SHORTCUT: You can access Death Mountain without completing all dungeons if you have the Power Bracelet.

SWORD BEAM: At full health, your sword fires a beam — always keep your hearts topped up!

🔫 CONTRA

KONAMI CODE: ↑↑↓↓←→←→BA + START = 30 lives! This is THE most famous cheat code in video game history.

SPREAD GUN: The most overpowered weapon — especially devastating against bosses when fired at close range.

2-PLAYER TIP: Player 2 can be sacrificed to let Player 1 safely collect power-ups — teamwork!
